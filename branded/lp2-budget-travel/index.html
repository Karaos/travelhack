How to Travel on a Budget

Tourist prices are optional.

What if half (or even MORE) of the accommodation price would magically disappear?

But the price of a trip isn't as fixed as it looks. The biggest line item in almost any travel budget is accommodation, often close to half the cost of the whole trip.
